How It Works

TympanIQ uses precisely calibrated sound frequencies to condition your auditory reflex pathways — the muscles that protect your inner ear from damage.

The Science

Your tensor tympani and stapedius muscles contract to shield your cochlea from loud sounds. Prolonged headphone use fatigues these reflexes. Our protocol gently re-engages them.

🎧

Optimized for AirPods

Binaural beats require stereo separation. TympanIQ is calibrated for in-ear devices, accounting for the occlusion effect and proximity to the tympanic membrane.

⚠️

Always start at low volume. Therapy is delivered at safe levels (50-75 dB). If you feel discomfort, stop immediately.
